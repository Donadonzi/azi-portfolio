Meca

Mobile Application Design

  • Product
  • UX
  • UI

A unique entertaining way to introduce public art to whom live or visit San Francisco. On Meca app, users engage with art subjects through the gaming experience.

Tools

Adobe Xd, Illustrator, Photoshop

Team

4 Designers

My Role

Project Coordinator, UX & UI Designer, Survey, Wireframing, Usability Testing

IMPACT

Public arts is free and accessible to everyone. There are tousands of public art pieces in San Francisco. It makes this city a great source of education through an amusement experience.

Problem

There is no comprehensive source for San Francisco residents to find and visit public artworks easily.

Solution

We designed a mobile application that entertain and educate users through AR gaming experience.

RESEARCH

TARGET AUDIENCE

Male and female under 45 who live in San Francisco with college education and upper. They have to own a smart phone which supports AR technology.

Primary Target Audience

- San Francisco new residents
- San Francisco Bay Area residents who are fan of art and technology

Secondary Target Audience

- People who lives in San Francisco
- Tourists and visitor
